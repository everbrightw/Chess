This is the \mbox{\hyperlink{classchess_1_1_pawn}{chess.\+Pawn}} object.

From Wikipedia, The pawn may move forward to the unoccupied square immediately in front of it on the same file; or on its first move it may advance two squares along the same file provided both squares are unoccupied; or it may move to a square occupied by an opponent\textquotesingle{}s piece which is diagonally in front of it on an adjacent file, capturing that piece.
